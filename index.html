About Me

I am a geophysicist specializing in the interaction between deep Earth processes and surface dynamics. My research focuses on megafloods, the Messinian Salinity Crisis, and the evolution of sedimentary basins. I develop numerical models to understand how tectonic and climate forces shape our planet over millions of years.

Megafloods & Lake Overflow

Studying the catastrophic release of water and its impact on landscape evolution and ocean circulation.

Messinian Salinity Crisis

Investigating the desiccation of the Mediterranean Sea and its environmental consequences.

Numerical Modeling

Developer of tAo and TISC software for lithospheric flexure and surface transport simulations.

Geodissemination

Promoting Earth Sciences through projects like the Augmented Reality Sandbox (sARndbox).
