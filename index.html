Planetary Geophysics Highlight

Mars Magmatic Events & Thermal Evolution

Using numerical modeling to decipher the volcanic and magmatic history of Mars. My work focuses on the thermal evolution of the Martian lithosphere and how surface heat flow relates to magmatic activity in regions like Elysium Planitia.
